About Moodie

Our Story

Moodie was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to bring high-quality entertainment to viewers around the world at an affordable price. What started as a small streaming service with a handful of titles has grown into a global platform with thousands of movies, series, and animated content.

Our journey has been driven by innovation, creativity, and a deep understanding of what viewers want. Today, we're proud to serve millions of users across the globe, offering them an unparalleled streaming experience.

Our Mission

At Moodie, we're committed to revolutionizing the way people experience entertainment. We believe in:

Accessibility: Making high-quality entertainment available to everyone, anywhere, anytime.

Innovation: Continuously improving our platform with cutting-edge technology.

Diversity: Offering a wide range of content that reflects different cultures, perspectives, and genres.

Quality: Providing an exceptional viewing experience with high-definition content and user-friendly interface.
