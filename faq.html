Account & Profile

How do I create a Moodie account?

Creating a Moodie account is easy! Visit our Sign Up page and follow these steps:

  1. Enter your email address and create a password
  2. Select your preferred subscription plan
  3. Enter your payment information
  4. Start enjoying unlimited movies and TV shows!
How do I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password, you can reset it by following these steps:

  1. Go to the Login page
  2. Click on "Forgot Password"
  3. Enter the email address associated with your account
  4. Check your email for password reset instructions
  5. Follow the link in the email to create a new password

For security reasons, password reset links expire after 24 hours.

How many profiles can I create per account?

The number of profiles you can create depends on your subscription plan:

  • Basic Plan: Up to 1 profile
  • Premium Plan: Up to 2 profiles
  • Ultimate Plan: Up to 4 profiles

Each profile allows for personalized recommendations and viewing history, making it easy for everyone in your household to have their own Moodie experience.

Can I change my email address?

Yes, you can change the email address associated with your Moodie account. To update your email:

  1. Log in to your Moodie account
  2. Go to "Account Settings"
  3. Click on "Edit" next to your email address
  4. Enter your new email address and password to confirm
  5. Click "Save Changes"

You'll receive a confirmation email at your new address. You'll need to click the verification link in that email to complete the change.

Subscription & Plans

What subscription plans does Moodie offer?

Moodie offers three subscription plans to meet different needs:

  • Basic Plan ($9.99/month): HD streaming on 1 device, unlimited movies and TV shows
  • Premium Plan ($14.99/month): Ultra HD streaming on 2 devices, unlimited movies and TV shows
  • Ultimate Plan ($19.99/month): 4K + HDR streaming on 4 devices, unlimited movies and TV shows

All plans allow you to watch on your laptop, TV, phone, or tablet, and you can cancel anytime. Visit our Pricing page for more details.

How do I change my subscription plan?

You can easily upgrade or downgrade your Moodie subscription plan at any time. To change your plan:

  1. Log in to your Moodie account
  2. Go to "Account Settings"
  3. Select "Subscription Plan"
  4. Choose "Change Plan"
  5. Select your new plan and confirm the change

If you upgrade, the change will take effect immediately. If you downgrade, the change will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

How do I cancel my subscription?

We're sorry to see you go! You can cancel your Moodie subscription at any time by following these steps:

  1. Log in to your Moodie account
  2. Go to "Account Settings"
  3. Select "Subscription Plan"
  4. Click "Cancel Subscription"
  5. Follow the prompts to confirm cancellation

Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing period. You will not be charged again unless you reactivate your subscription.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes, Moodie offers a 7-day free trial for new subscribers. During the trial period, you'll have full access to all features of your selected plan.

To sign up for a free trial:

  1. Go to our Sign Up page
  2. Select your preferred plan
  3. Create your account
  4. Enter your payment information (you won't be charged until the trial ends)

You can cancel at any time during the trial period and you won't be charged. If you continue after the trial period, your subscription will automatically begin and you'll be billed according to your selected plan.

Viewing & Content

How can I find specific movies or shows?

There are several ways to find content on Moodie:

  • Search: Use the search bar at the top of the screen to search for titles, actors, directors, or genres
  • Browse: Explore our categories like Movies, Series, or Cartoons
  • Recommendations: Check your personalized recommendations on the home page
  • New Releases: View our "Recently Added" section for the latest content

You can also use filters to narrow down your search by genre, release year, or rating.

Can I download content to watch offline?

Yes, all Moodie subscription plans allow you to download content for offline viewing on our mobile apps for iOS and Android devices.

To download content:

  1. Open the Moodie app on your mobile device
  2. Find the movie or episode you want to download
  3. Tap the download icon

Downloaded content is available for 7 days or until 48 hours after you start watching. The number of downloads allowed varies by subscription plan:

  • Basic Plan: Up to 5 downloads
  • Premium Plan: Up to 10 downloads
  • Ultimate Plan: Up to 20 downloads
What devices can I watch Moodie on?

Moodie is compatible with a wide range of devices, including:

  • Smart TVs (Samsung, LG, Sony, etc.)
  • Streaming devices (Roku, Amazon Fire TV, Apple TV, Chromecast)
  • Game consoles (PlayStation, Xbox)
  • Mobile devices (iOS and Android phones and tablets)
  • Computers (via web browsers)

To start watching, simply download the Moodie app on your device or visit Moodie.com in your web browser.

How often is new content added?

We add new movies, series, and cartoons to our library every week. Our content team is constantly working to bring you the latest releases and classic favorites.

To stay updated on new additions:

  • Check the "Recently Added" section on the home page
  • Follow us on social media for announcements
  • Enable notifications in your account settings to receive alerts about new content

We also feature special collections around holidays and major events.

Technical Issues

What can I do if videos won't play or keep buffering?

If you're experiencing playback issues, try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Check your internet connection speed (we recommend at least 3 Mbps for SD, 5 Mbps for HD, and 15 Mbps for 4K)
  2. Restart your device and router
  3. Close other applications or browser tabs that might be using bandwidth
  4. Lower the video quality in the player settings
  5. Clear your browser cache or app data
  6. Try a different device or browser

If problems persist, please contact our support team with details about your device, internet connection, and the specific content you're trying to watch.

How do I fix audio/video sync issues?

If you're experiencing audio and video that are out of sync, try these solutions:

  1. Pause the video for 10-15 seconds and then resume playback
  2. Refresh the page or restart the app
  3. Check if your device's operating system and the Moodie app are up to date
  4. Try lowering the video quality
  5. If using a browser, try a different one
  6. Check your audio settings and make sure there's no delay enabled

If the issue persists across multiple titles, please contact our support team for assistance.

Why can't I see content in 4K/HDR?

To stream content in 4K/HDR, you need:

  1. An Ultimate subscription plan (our Basic and Premium plans don't support 4K)
  2. A 4K/HDR compatible device
  3. A strong internet connection (at least 15 Mbps)
  4. Content that is available in 4K/HDR (look for the 4K or HDR badge)

Additionally, make sure:

  • Your display is 4K/HDR compatible
  • You're using an HDMI cable that supports HDCP 2.2 (for external devices)
  • Your streaming quality is set to "High" in your account settings

Note that not all content is available in 4K/HDR, even with an Ultimate subscription.

How do I update the Moodie app?

To ensure the best streaming experience, it's important to keep your Moodie app updated. Here's how to update on different devices:

  • iOS devices: Open the App Store, tap on your profile picture, scroll down to see available updates, and tap "Update" next to the Moodie app
  • Android devices: Open the Google Play Store, tap on your profile icon, select "Manage apps & device," and update Moodie if available
  • Smart TVs: Most Smart TVs update apps automatically. You can also check for updates in your TV's app store
  • Streaming devices: Follow the device-specific instructions for Roku, Apple TV, Fire TV, etc.

For web browsers, Moodie automatically uses the latest version when you refresh the page.

Billing & Payment

What payment methods do you accept?

Moodie accepts the following payment methods:

  • Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
  • Debit cards (with Visa or Mastercard logo)
  • PayPal
  • Gift cards and promotional codes
  • Select mobile carrier billing (depending on your location)

Payment methods may vary by location. You can view and update your payment method in your account settings at any time.

When will I be charged for my subscription?

Your Moodie subscription is charged at the beginning of each billing cycle. For new subscribers who sign up for the 7-day free trial, the first charge occurs when the trial period ends.

For monthly subscriptions, you'll be charged on the same date each month. If you subscribed on the 31st of a month, and a subsequent month has fewer days, you'll be charged on the last day of that month.

You can find your next billing date in your account settings under "Subscription and Billing."

How do I update my payment information?

To update your payment information:

  1. Log in to your Moodie account
  2. Go to "Account Settings"
  3. Select "Subscription and Billing"
  4. Click "Update Payment Method"
  5. Enter your new payment details and save changes

Your updated payment method will be used for your next billing cycle. If your current payment method has expired or been declined, updating your information will trigger an immediate charge to reactivate your subscription.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied with the service?

Moodie subscriptions are prepaid and non-refundable for partial billing periods. However, we evaluate refund requests on a case-by-case basis for issues such as:

  • Technical problems that significantly impacted your ability to use the service
  • Accidental or duplicate charges
  • Free trial billing errors

If you believe you're entitled to a refund, please contact our support team with your account details and the reason for your request. We aim to resolve all issues fairly and promptly.

Remember that you can cancel your subscription at any time, and you'll still have access to Moodie until the end of your current billing period.
